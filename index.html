Bio

I am a Research Assistant at National University of Singapore (NUS) in the CVML@NUS group working with Angela Yao and Fadime Sener.

I completed my bachelors in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University, India where I have worked with Sanjoy Kumar Saha and Ananda S. Chowdhury. My bachelor's thesis was on "Open-Set Metric Learning for Person Re-identification in the Wild" (link).

Broadly, I am interested in computer vision and machine learning for video understanding. Currently, I am working on action anticipation, hand-object interaction detection and 3D action recognition.
